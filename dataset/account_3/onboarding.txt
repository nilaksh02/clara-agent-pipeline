I just want to confirm a few of the details for our call handling setup.

Our office hours are Monday through Friday from 9 AM to 6 PM Eastern time.

During those hours the assistant should collect the caller’s name, phone number,
and a short description of the plumbing issue before forwarding the request to
our scheduling team.

Emergency situations include pipe bursts, major water leaks, or flooding inside
the home.

If a caller reports an emergency plumbing issue, the assistant should attempt to
transfer the call directly to the on-call plumber.

If the transfer cannot be completed, the assistant should reassure the caller
that someone from our team will return their call shortly and the request should
be marked as urgent.

For non-emergency requests, the assistant should simply log the information so
our office staff can schedule the job.
