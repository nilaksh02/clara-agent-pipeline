Thanks again for helping us set up the AI assistant for Northgate Electrical Services.

I just want to confirm a few of the details we discussed earlier during the demo call.

Our official office hours are Monday through Friday from 8 AM to 5 PM Eastern time.
During those hours the assistant should greet the caller, collect their name and
phone number, and ask what electrical issue they are experiencing.

Once that information is collected, the request should be routed to our dispatcher
so that a technician can be scheduled.

Emergency situations include electrical hazards, burning smells from outlets,
sparking equipment, or partial power outages inside the property.

If the assistant detects one of those situations, it should collect the caller’s
contact information and service address and attempt to transfer the call to the
technician on duty.

If the technician does not answer the transfer request, the assistant should inform
the caller that someone from our team will call them back shortly and mark the
service request as urgent.

We also want to make sure the assistant does not automatically create work orders
in our internal system. Those should still be reviewed by our dispatcher first.
