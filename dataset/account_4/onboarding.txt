Thanks again for helping us set up the AI answering assistant for Horizon Climate Systems.

I just want to go over a few of the operational details so the assistant handles calls the way our office normally does.

Our business hours are Monday through Friday from 8 AM to 5 PM Eastern time. During those hours the assistant should greet callers, ask how it can help, and collect the caller’s name and phone number.

After collecting the basic contact information, the assistant should ask the caller to briefly describe the HVAC problem they are experiencing.

Most of the calls we receive are for routine issues like air conditioning repairs, seasonal HVAC maintenance, or installation consultations. Those calls should simply be logged and passed to our dispatcher so they can schedule an appointment.

However, we do occasionally receive emergency calls.

For our business, emergency situations include complete heating failure during winter months or total air conditioning failure during periods of extreme heat.

If the assistant detects that the caller is reporting an emergency HVAC issue, it should collect the caller’s name, phone number, and service address and attempt to transfer the call directly to the on-call technician.

If the transfer attempt fails or the technician does not answer, the assistant should reassure the caller that someone from our team will return their call shortly and mark the request as urgent.

Also, we do not want the assistant automatically creating service tickets in our internal dispatch software. The dispatcher will review and create the work order manually after reviewing the call details.
